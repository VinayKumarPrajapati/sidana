The Fusion of Tech & Music

As the CEO of HireQuotient and a passionate music producer, I've found that innovation thrives at the intersection of logic and artistry. My journey from IIT Delhi to Boston Consulting Group has shaped both my business acumen and creative vision.

Music serves as my creative sanctuary - a space where algorithms meet melodies, where data transforms into emotion, and where the precision of technology enhances the beauty of human expression.

Through my compositions, I explore themes of love, technology, and human connection, creating soundscapes that resonate with both the heart and mind.

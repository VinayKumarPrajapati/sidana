The Intersection of Innovation & Artistry

My journey spans the analytical rigor of IIT Delhi engineering, the strategic thinking of Boston Consulting Group, and the creative expression of music and poetry. As CEO of HireQuotient, I'm revolutionizing global talent acquisition through AI-powered solutions, while my artistic pursuits in music production and shayari writing provide a creative balance to entrepreneurial life.

This unique combination of technical expertise, business acumen, and artistic sensibility allows me to approach challenges from multiple perspectives. Whether I'm building AI algorithms that transform hiring processes, composing melodies that touch hearts, or crafting verses that capture emotional depths, the common thread is innovation powered by passion.

Music, poetry, and technology aren't separate worlds to me - they're interconnected expressions of creativity, each informing and enhancing the others in the pursuit of meaningful impact.
